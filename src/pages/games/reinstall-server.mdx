# How to Reinstall your Game Server
<Steps>
### Login to Panel
First login on [https://panel.forestracks.com](https://panel.forestracks.com) and click on your server. If you cannot login, follow the [Troubleshooting Guide](/games/troubleshoot/invalid-login).
### Create a Backup
Next click the "Backups" panel tab and click "Create Backup"
### Delete Current Save
Click the "File Manager" tab and delete everything
### Run Reinstall
Click the "Settings" tab and click the "Reinstall Server" button.
</Steps>
